This is a basement/garden level apartment with access to a workshop and backyard. It is situated on a quiet street in Ridgewood, 4 minutes from the Fresh Pond Road station.

The kitchen features a vintage gas stove, spacious butcher block counters and a massive barn-style kitchen sink. Appliances include a family-size stainless steel Electrolux fridge and a Miele dishwasher.

The wall between the bedroom and the living room has a big framed window, in which we’ve installed a projector screen so that it’s possible to watch movies on the big screen from both the bedroom and the living room.

Towards the back of the apartment is a half bath and a separate free standing clawfoot tub, outfitted with a shower.

The backyard and woodshop is accessed through a sliding door, and is shared only with the residents of the first floor apartment. In the woodshop, there are some tools—table saw, mitre saw, drill press, bandsaw, planer and a bunch of cordless power tools, to name a few—that you are welcome to use (safely & at your own risk!). The backyard is about 525sqft, with shared gas and charcoal grills as well as a chiminea.
